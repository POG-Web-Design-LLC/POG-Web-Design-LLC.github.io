About Stone Church

At Stone Church, a proud division of Gallagher Concrete Construction, we embrace a passionate vision: that every creation should stand the test of time. With a reverence akin to the awe-inspiring architecture of the ancient Greeks, we pour our hearts into building not just structures, but legacies. We construct each project with unwavering dedication, ensuring that it’s done the right way, the very best way—crafted to endure and inspire for generations to come.
